About
Overview

We present a new, intuitive, interactive and efficient way to explore medical imaging data. A typical T1-weighted brain MRI is loaded in this demo. The displayed slices are computed in real-time with pure JavaScript.

Is it useful?

Yes.
Often times, medical professionals want to look at the data in arbitrary directions. Established software do not allow user to explore the data in a friendly manner.

The image

Modality: T1-weighted MRI
Anatomy: Brain
Size: 256 x 256 x 176 pixels
Pixel: 32 bits

UI Help
You can (and should!:)) interact with all the elements in the UI

General panel

Interaction mode:
Demo: Reslice data randomly
Cam: Reslice data in camera direction
Box: Reslice data based on slice orientation normals directions
Show BBox:Show/Hide the data bounding box
Slice Coloring:Turn on/off slice coloring based on slice normal direction

Slice Orientation

Normal X Dir: X component of the slice normal
Normal Y Dir: Y component of the slice normal
Normal Z Dir: Z component of the slice normal
Color: Color based on the slice normal RBG(NormZ,NormY,NormX)

Slice Selection

Slice index: Slice location among existing slices in slice normal direction

Scene Orientation

View
Free: User defines camera location
Sagittal: Look at the data sagittally
Coronal: Look at the data coronally
Axial: Look at the data axially
